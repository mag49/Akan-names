AKAN NAME GENERATOR

Akan names are derived from the Ghanian culture.A new-born's name
is derived from the day of the week they were born,
male and females are assigned separate names depending on their gender.
you can fill out the form we have here to identify your name
according to Ghanian culture.

This website takes the gender, date,month
and birth year of the user and generates
the Akan name according to Ghana naming method.
